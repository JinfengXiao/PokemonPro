What is Pokemon Pro?

Pokemon Pro is a website that has the tools that will enable you to become a Pokemaster. Whether you are playing on a Nintendo Switch, playing cards, or PokemonGo, the concept is the same. You can search for your favorite pokemon in three ways: using the search page linked at the left, entering a SQL query in the first text box below, or entering a keyword or natural language query in the second text box below. You tell us what features you would like in a Pokemon and we will tell you your perfect match. You can even create your own imaginatory pokemon, or play a small pokemon battle game!
